My name is Sepideh Abedini. I am a master’s student at the University of Waterloo, under the supervision of Prof. M. Tamer Özsu at the Data Systems Group. Currently an Associate Applied ML Engineer at the Vector Institute, developing privacy-aware text-to-SQL and PII detection pipelines using abstraction techniques, LLMs, and synthetic data.

My research focuses on the application of LLMs in Data Management Systems and Data Privacy.
